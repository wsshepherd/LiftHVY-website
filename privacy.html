Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Account info: email, profile details
  • Workout data: activities, GPS, heart‐rate from Strava/Garmin
  • Chat data: your messages & AI replies
  • Device data: crash reports, iOS version

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To power your dashboard & chatbot
  • To fetch & display Strava/Garmin activities
  • To improve the app via anonymized analytics

3. Third-Party Sharing

  • We only share with Strava, Garmin, and OpenAI to fetch data or generate responses.
  • We never sell or rent your personal info.

4. Storage & Security

Tokens are in your iOS Keychain, all traffic is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS), no server‐side storage.

5. Your Choices

Disconnect or uninstall to delete all local data. Email support@rnhvy.app for questions.
